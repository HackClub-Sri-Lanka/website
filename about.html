about us

A club, not a course.

Hack Club Sri Lanka is part of the global Hack Club network — a home for teenagers who'd rather build a working prototype at 2am than sit through another slide deck.

We meet weekly, hack on real projects, and send our best builders to hackathons around the world. There are no prerequisites, no fees, and no gatekeeping — if you can hold a laptop, you can join.

Hack Club Sri Lanka is entirely teen-run. The people who plan workshops, review project submissions, and manage the community are members themselves — usually still doing their O/Levels or A/Levels between commits.

🆓

Free, always

LKR 0 to join, LKR 0 for materials at workshops. Funded by Hack Club HQ and sponsors — never by members.
