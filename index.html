01 · what we are

A club, not a course.

Hack Club Sri Lanka is part of the global Hack Club network — a home for teenagers who'd rather build a working prototype at 2am than sit through another slide deck. We meet weekly, hack on real projects, and send our best builders to hackathons around the world. No prerequisites. No fees. No gatekeeping.
